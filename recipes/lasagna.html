Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

It’s hearty, delicious, and pairs perfectly with a slice of garlic bread and a glass of red wine.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook the ground beef and onion in a pan until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 30–40 minutes until golden.
  6. Let it rest before serving. Enjoy!
