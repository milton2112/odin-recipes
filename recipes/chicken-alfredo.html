Chicken Alfredo

Description

A creamy pasta dish with tender chicken and rich Alfredo sauce. Easy, comforting, and perfect for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the chicken until golden.
  3. Add butter, cream, and Parmesan to create the sauce.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and chicken. Serve hot!
