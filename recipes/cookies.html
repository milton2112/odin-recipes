Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies that everyone loves. Simple to make and impossible to resist!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix butter and sugars until creamy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, then flour and baking soda.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake 10–12 minutes until golden.
